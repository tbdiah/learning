Tuberculosis Monitoring, Evaluation and Surveillance

Using a Performance-based M&E Framework to Strengthen TB Programming

This online learning experience is designed for dedicated professionals working to end TB in their countries. It provides essential information and guidance on how to effectively monitor and evaluate the performance of TB programs using a performance-based framework. This course requires a basic understanding of TB M&E and surveillance systems and explores how a performance-based M&E framework with standardized, high-quality indicators can improve the generation of quality data and support the analysis, interpretation and use of that data to inform programming, identify gaps, and strengthen the overall system.

The TB DIAH project is part of USAID's performance-based measures to end TB—the Global Accelerator to End Tuberculosis. The  TB DIAH website features a data hub, guidance, and tools to support the work of the global TB community. TB DIAH makes possible optimal analysis and use of TB data to inform national TB programs, policies, and USAID-supported interventions.

This information was produced with the support of the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) under the terms of the TB Data, Impact Assessment and Communications Hub (TB DIAH) Associate Award No. 7200AA18LA00007.

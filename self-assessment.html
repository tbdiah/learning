TB M&E e-Learning: Self-Assessment

Using a Performance-based M&E Framework to Strengthen TB Programming


Course Home » Self- Assessment

TIME TO COMPLETE: 5-10 minutes

Please take a few minutes to provide some details about yourself and complete a brief self-assessment to prepare you for the online course. You will also have an opportunity to establish personal learning goals and explore hand-picked resources related to TB M&E and surveillance.

Note: It may take a few seconds to load depending on your network connection.


The TB DIAH project is part of USAID's performance-based measures to end TB—the Global Accelerator to End Tuberculosis. The  TB DIAH website features a data hub, guidance, and tools to support the work of the global TB community. TB DIAH makes possible optimal analysis and use of TB data to inform national TB programs, policies, and USAID-supported interventions.
